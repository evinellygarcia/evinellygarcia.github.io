Smoothie Store Customer Queue Management

A program to manage and organizes a.

Description

This project simulates customer queuing and service management in a smoothie store operating with limited staff. Originally staffed with multiple clerks across 12 counters, the store now runs with only one clerk due to unforeseen circumstances. To maintain the appearance of normal operations, customers are distributed into multiple queues. Each customer is recorded with their arrival time, last name, line number, and number of smoothies to purchase. When serving, the clerk selects the next customer by prioritizing the one with the fewest smoothies; ties are resolved by choosing the lowest-numbered line. Service time is calculated as a base of 30 seconds plus 5 seconds per smoothie. The system models how queue structures, prioritization rules, and service time calculations affect customer flow and overall store efficiency.
